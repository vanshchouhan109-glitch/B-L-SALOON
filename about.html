Our Story

About Brown Luxury

Meet Dulcelina

Your Personal Foot Care Expert

Welcome to Brown Luxury, a premium pedicure salon in the heart of Berkel en Rodenrijs. The salon is owned and operated by Dulce Duque — affectionately known as Dulcelina — whose warm, caring approach and absolute professionalism have earned her a loyal following across South Holland.

Dulce's philosophy is simple: a pedicure should never be rushed, never be ordinary, and should always leave you feeling cared for. She brings over 15 years of hands-on expertise, combining medical knowledge with a genuine passion for beauty and wellness.

Every tool is sterilised. Every product is premium. Every client is treated like a valued guest. That is the Brown Luxury promise.
